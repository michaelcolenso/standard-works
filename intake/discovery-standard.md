# Discovery Standard — Knowledge Products Only

This standard governs the creation of:

- downloadable info products
- playbooks, templates, frameworks
- calculators, worksheets, decision tools
- research briefings and digests

Standard Works does not currently produce software products.

All gates are mandatory.
Failure at any gate terminates analysis.


## Discovery Framework

### Phase 0: Niche Discovery
Identify opportunities where information asymmetry creates urgent problems:

- Regulatory/compliance changes
- Platform algorithm shifts
- Tool complexity explosions
- Career transition waves
- Expensive service alternatives
- Fragmented tribal knowledge

### Phase 1: Spending Signal Archaeology
Search communities (Reddit, Discord, forums) for evidence of active spending:

- "paid for"
- "course on"
- "wasted money on"
- "hired someone to"
- "finally bought"

**Quality threshold:** Minimum 3 signals with specific dollar amounts.

### Phase 2: Information Gap Analysis
Document what users cannot find:

- Specific unanswered questions
- Contradictory advice
- Outdated methods
- Time/cost of current workarounds

### Phase 3: Viability Gates (All Must Pass)

| Gate | Test |
|------|------|
| Solo Creatable | Can one person complete in 2-4 weeks? |
| Information Accessible | Researchable through public sources? |
| AI Leverage | Enables superior depth, speed, or personalization? |
| Distribution | Reachable audience without paid ads? |
| Format Fit | Maps to playbook, template, or framework? |

### Phase 4: Opportunity Scoring (1-5 Scale)

Score on spending clarity, information gap, urgency, create feasibility, and AI multiplier.

**Decision thresholds:**
- 22-25: Create immediately
- 18-21: Strong—proceed with pre-sale
- 14-17: Moderate—requires narrower focus
- <14: Pass or pivot

### Phase 5: DISQUALIFIERS (Abandon If Any Apply)

- No specific dollar amounts in spending signals
- General curiosity, not urgent/costly
- Requires months of research or expert credentials
- Comprehensive free alternatives exist
- No identifiable first 100 buyers
- Topic changes too fast to maintain relevance
