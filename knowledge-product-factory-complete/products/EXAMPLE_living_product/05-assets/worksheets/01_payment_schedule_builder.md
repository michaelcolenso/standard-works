# Payment Schedule Builder (Worksheet)

Outcome:
Produce a milestone schedule that preserves leverage.

Steps:
1) Set upfront cap (default ≤10%).
2) List inspections and map milestones to “verification points”.
3) Add final holdback (retainage) until punchlist complete.

Example:
Upfront 5%; final holdback 10%.

Verification:
No milestone is payable without evidence.
