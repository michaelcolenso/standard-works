# Problem Definition — Permit-Driven Home Projects (Example)

## Buyer job-to-be-done
When I’m planning a permit-driven home project (ADU/addition/major remodel), I want a reliable decision system and toolkit so I can avoid delays, rework, and contractor disputes.

## Pain & stakes
- Delays cascade into financing extensions, remobilization, and stress.
- Bad sequencing causes redesign fees and change orders.
- Free advice is inconsistent and local rules change.

## Alternatives & failures
- Blog posts: fragmented, not executable.
- City pages: accurate but incomplete for end-to-end workflow.
- Consultants: expensive; buyers want DIY clarity first.
