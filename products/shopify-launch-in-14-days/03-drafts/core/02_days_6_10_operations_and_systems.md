# Days 6–10: Operations and Systems Readiness (Core)

Outcome:
Enable reliable transaction flow and store operations so customers can discover products, check out successfully, and receive predictable post-purchase handling.

Steps:
1) Day 6 — Run storefront UX sanity checks for navigation, collections, search, and mobile readability.
2) Day 7 — Configure payment providers, checkout settings, abandoned checkout basics, and order confirmation messaging.
3) Day 8 — Set shipping profiles and rates by product class/region with explicit handling and transit windows.
4) Day 9 — Complete tax baseline and legal/policy consistency review across checkout and storefront pages.
5) Day 10 — Install only must-have apps with defined use cases (e.g., reviews, email capture, basic analytics extension) and document each app's expected outcome.

Example:
A side hustler launching a skincare store discovers on Day 6 that collection filters hide top products on mobile. They fix the collection structure before paid traffic. On Day 10, they install only reviews + email capture and defer upsell tools to week 2.

Verification:
- Test order can be completed using at least one real payment method in test-safe workflow.
- Shipping charge displayed at checkout matches documented shipping policy scenarios.
- Every installed app has one owner, one success metric, and one removal rule if unused.
