# Risks — shopify-launch-in-14-days

## Delivery risks
- Risk: Scope expansion turns a practical playbook into a broad course.
  - Mitigation: enforce scope guardrails and replacement rule for any new section.
- Risk: Product remains too abstract despite detailed spec.
  - Mitigation: require every chapter to include action outputs and verification checks.

## Market risks
- Risk: Audience resists paid product due to abundant free content.
  - Mitigation: position around execution speed, decision clarity, and mistake prevention.
- Risk: Price-position mismatch for first-time founders.
  - Mitigation: test entry price points and iterate offer framing from early buyer feedback.

## Maintenance risks
- Risk: Shopify interface/process updates cause content drift.
  - Mitigation: maintain updates log, monthly audit, and patch cadence.
