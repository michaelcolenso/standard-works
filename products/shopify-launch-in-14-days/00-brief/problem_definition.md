# Problem Definition

## Buyer job-to-be-done
Launch a credible Shopify store quickly with the minimum required setup to start taking orders, while avoiding technical mistakes that hurt trust and conversion.

## Pain & stakes
- Too many setup decisions create analysis paralysis.
- New owners over-focus on visual tweaks and under-invest in offer clarity, trust elements, and checkout flow.
- Time is wasted on low-impact tasks, delaying launch and first revenue.
- Missing core setup steps (payments, policies, analytics, shipping rules) causes preventable conversion and operations failures.

## Alternatives & failures
- YouTube tutorials are fragmented and often outdated.
- Shopify docs are accurate but not prioritized for first-launch sequencing.
- Courses are expensive and broad, with slow implementation cycles.
- Agencies are out of budget for most first-time founders.

## Desired transformation
From "overwhelmed and stuck" to "launched in 14 days with a validated, conversion-ready baseline and clear first-week optimization plan."
