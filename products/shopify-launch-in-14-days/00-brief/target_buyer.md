# Target Buyer

## Primary buyer
First-time Shopify store owners and side hustlers launching a first ecommerce brand with limited time, limited technical confidence, and a strong need for execution clarity.

## Top pains
- Unsure what to do first and in what order
- Fear of missing critical launch steps
- Decision fatigue from themes/apps/settings overload
- Spending too much time on non-essential tasks

## Not for
- Experienced ecommerce operators with established SOPs
- Agencies and consultants needing enterprise workflows
- Store owners seeking advanced post-scale optimization only
