# Kill Criteria

Kill after two loops if:
- No clear evidence of willingness to pay for a launch checklist product at $47+
- No meaningful differentiation from free Shopify launch content
- Audience acquisition channels (Reddit/forums/organic content) show weak reach or poor engagement
- Product cannot provide at least 5 concrete reusable assets superior to free alternatives
- Scope cannot be contained to a 3–4 week build with high confidence
