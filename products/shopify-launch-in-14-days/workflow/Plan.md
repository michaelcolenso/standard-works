# Plan

Milestones:
1. **Brief lock (Phase 0)**
   - Acceptance: buyer, pain, pricing, and kill criteria are explicit and consistent with `spec.md`.
2. **Research confidence (Phase 1)**
   - Acceptance: source index, summaries, and contradictions identify highest-risk launch mistakes and decision bottlenecks.
3. **Architecture freeze (Phase 2)**
   - Acceptance: final 14-day outline, asset map, and scope guardrails approved.
4. **Core drafting (Phase 3)**
   - Acceptance: complete draft content for all 14 days + supporting frameworks.
5. **Validation pass (Phase 4)**
   - Acceptance: adversarial review issues resolved and revision log complete.
6. **Asset pack completion (Phase 5)**
   - Acceptance: 5–7 reusable assets created and verified.
7. **Packaging + launch readiness (Phases 6–7)**
   - Acceptance: quick start, release notes, launch messaging, objections, and distribution plan complete.

Rule: stop-and-fix if any gate fails.
