# Prompt

Use this file as the immutable project specification.

## Goals
- Build a practical knowledge product for first-time Shopify merchants that enables a 14-day launch with clear sequencing.
- Minimize buyer overwhelm through decision frameworks, templates, and checklists.
- Ship an MVP in 3–4 weeks at quality high enough for paid distribution.

## Non-goals
- Building a broad “all of ecommerce” course.
- Covering advanced scale-stage CRO or media buying in v1.

## Hard constraints
- Keep the buyer definition narrow (first-time Shopify launchers).
- Prioritize execution assets over theory.
- Maintain phase order and pass gate checks before advancing.
- Capture all decisions and progress in repo files.

## Deliverables
- `spec.md` with product strategy and scope.
- Completed phase docs from 00-brief through 07-launch (MVP depth).
- 5–7 reusable launch assets (checklists/templates/timelines).
- Packaging outputs suitable for sale.

## Done when
- Product can be executed by a new store owner without additional coaching.
- `scripts/status_check.py` passes for the product directory.
- Validation and launch prep artifacts are complete enough for first sales test.
