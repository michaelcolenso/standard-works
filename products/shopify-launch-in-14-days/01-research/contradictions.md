# Contradictions

## Contradiction 1
- Claim: “Pick the perfect theme before anything else.”
- Counter-signal: First-store failures are more often caused by missing payment/shipping/trust setup than imperfect visuals.
- Product decision: Delay deep theme customization until conversion and operations baseline is complete.

## Contradiction 2
- Claim: “Install many apps early to future-proof.”
- Counter-signal: Early app overload increases complexity, slows store speed, and confuses setup priorities.
- Product decision: enforce must-have-first app sequence and defer nice-to-have apps until post-launch week 1.

## Contradiction 3
- Claim: “You need paid ads ready before launch.”
- Counter-signal: First launch bottleneck is often store readiness and messaging clarity, not channel sophistication.
- Product decision: include minimal post-launch marketing sequence focused on owned/organic actions first.

## Contradiction 4
- Claim: “Shopify docs already solve this problem.”
- Counter-signal: Docs are feature-specific, not outcome-sequenced for novice operators.
- Product decision: build day-by-day dependency map that translates docs into execution order.
