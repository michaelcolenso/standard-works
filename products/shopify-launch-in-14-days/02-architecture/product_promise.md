# Product Promise

## Primary promise
Launch a conversion-ready Shopify store in 14 days by following a daily execution plan that prioritizes high-impact setup tasks and eliminates low-value busywork.

## Specific outcomes
By the end of the playbook, the buyer will have:
- a live storefront with clear offer and product pages,
- configured payments, shipping, taxes, and policy/trust pages,
- a lightweight must-have app stack,
- baseline analytics/tracking for post-launch decisions,
- a launch-day QA pass and first 7-day optimization workflow.

## Proof mechanism
- Daily deliverables with pass/fail checks
- Pre-launch verification checklist
- Common mistake warnings paired with direct fixes

## Boundary statement
This product is for first launch execution, not advanced growth-stage optimization.
