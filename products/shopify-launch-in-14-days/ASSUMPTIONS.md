# Assumptions — shopify-launch-in-14-days

## Market and buyer assumptions
1. First-time Shopify founders will pay $47–$97 to save 2–3 weeks of trial-and-error setup time.
2. The strongest buying trigger is overwhelm reduction through explicit sequencing, not additional theory.
3. Organic acquisition can be generated via forum-driven pain-point content and checklist-led CTA.

## Product assumptions
4. A 50–60 page core guide plus 5–7 assets is sufficient for immediate execution without coaching.
5. A strict must-have vs nice-to-have framework will reduce scope creep and increase completion rates.
6. Most first-launch failures can be prevented by front-loading trust/checkout/operations setup.

## Validation plan
- Test willingness-to-pay through preorder/waitlist offers and direct outreach.
- Interview initial buyers on which sections unlocked the most progress.
- Track completion and refund reasons to identify weak assumptions.
