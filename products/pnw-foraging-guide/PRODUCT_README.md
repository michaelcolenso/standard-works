# pnw-foraging-guide

**PNW Foraging & Mycology Field Guide + Log System**

A 60-page offline-capable PDF field guide covering the 50 most frequently encountered Pacific Northwest wild edibles (35 fungi, 15 plants), bundled with 5 interconnected templates: Harvest Log, Safety Checklist, Seasonal Calendar, Recipe Quick-Ref, and a Verification Sheet.

Target buyer: serious PNW weekend foragers, 1–5 years experience, who need a binary identification system and a structured harvest log — without relying on cell signal.

**Confidence rating: HIGH** — strong regional demand signal, proven Etsy spend, clear differentiation gap vs. existing options.
