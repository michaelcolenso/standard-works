# Metrics — pnw-foraging-guide

## Pre-launch quality gates (all must pass before listing goes live)

- 0 language lint violations (no ambiguity blacklist terms without a decision rule)
- 0 species profiles with unresolved adversarial review flags
- >= 3 worked examples per ID gate format (at least 3 species fully walked through from specimen to harvest/abort decision)
- >= 5 assets complete (Harvest Log, Safety Checklist, Seasonal Calendar, Recipe Quick-Ref, Verification Sheet)
- Harvest Log setup time <= 10 minutes (validated by 2 external testers)
- PDFs build successfully in dist/ (Core Guide, Quick Start, Asset Pack)
- Mandatory disclaimer language present on cover page and on every species profile with a deadly look-alike

## Demand validation (free sample phase — target: Days 1–14 after drop)

| Signal | Target | Kill threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Free sample downloads (r/foraging + r/mycology) | 200 in 14 days | Under 50 in 14 days → rewrite positioning |
| Email/follow captures from sample page | 30 in 14 days | Under 10 → audit CTA copy |
| Unsolicited "where can I buy this?" comments | 5 in 14 days | 0 → delay paid launch; run second free test |

## Post-launch conversion metrics (target: first 60 days)

| Metric | Target | Action threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Sample → paid conversion rate | >= 2% | Under 1% → revise pricing or listing copy |
| Refund rate | < 5% | Over 5% → audit template usability |
| Etsy listing views | 500 in 30 days | Under 100 → revise tags and title |
| Etsy listing click-through rate | >= 3% | Under 1% → revise thumbnail and first image |
| Total units sold (30 days) | >= 20 at $19 = $380 | Under 10 units → reassess channel and pricing |

## Revenue targets

| Milestone | Revenue | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Break-even (production cost recovery) | $150 | Day 30 |
| Proof of concept | $500 | Day 60 |
| Sustainable | $1,000/month recurring | Month 6 (with seasonal repeat buyers) |

## Living product update metrics

- Time-to-patch when a species taxonomy or regulation changes: <= 14 days from signal
- Annual update digest released before March 1 each year (ahead of spring season)
