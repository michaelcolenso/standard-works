# Assumptions — pnw-foraging-guide

Each assumption has a test and a stated consequence if wrong.

## A1: Buyers will pay $19 for a PDF + spreadsheet bundle

**Assumption:** PNW foragers who currently pay $15–$28 for Etsy mushroom journals will pay $19 for a bundle that adds safety decision trees and a harvest log.
**Test:** Free sample PDF drives 200+ downloads in 14 days; 2% of downloaders convert to $19 purchase within 30 days = 4 sales minimum.
**If wrong:** Drop to $14 and test for 30 more days. If still under 2% conversion, narrow scope and reposition as log-only at $9.

## A2: r/foraging and r/mycology allow product-adjacent posts via free sample drops

**Assumption:** Posting a genuine free resource (5-species PDF, no overt promotion) to these subreddits will not be removed and will drive organic traffic.
**Test:** Post and monitor for 48 hours. If removed, do not repost. Route to PNW-specific Facebook groups and mycology club forums instead.
**If wrong:** Adjust distribution plan to Etsy SEO-primary + club email lists. Reddit is a bonus channel, not the only one.

## A3: A single advisor review pass (PSMS) is sufficient for "expert-reviewed" claims

**Assumption:** One PSMS chapter advisor reviewing and signing off on species profiles is sufficient to use "expert-reviewed" in marketing copy.
**Test:** Check PSMS's own published guidelines on what constitutes an endorsed product. If they prohibit endorsements from members, do not use the language — use "peer-reviewed by a PNW mycologist" with their permission.
**If wrong:** Launch without the claim. Add it post-launch if a formal review is arranged.

## A4: Excel + Google Sheets dual format covers 90% of the target buyer's tool preference

**Assumption:** The target buyer uses Excel or Google Sheets, not Notion or Airtable, as their primary tabular tool for personal tracking.
**Test:** One-question poll embedded in free sample download confirmation page; 50 responses within 30 days.
**If wrong:** If Notion exceeds 30% of responses, build Notion template as an equivalent primary deliverable (not just a bonus).

## A5: 50 species is achievable at production quality within a 3-week drafting window

**Assumption:** One author + LLM assistance can produce 50 accurate, adversarially-reviewed species profiles in 15–21 days.
**Test:** Complete 10 profiles in the first 3 days. If any profile takes more than 45 minutes, adjust scope to 35 species for v1.0.
**If wrong:** Cut to 35 species for v1.0; create a "Season 2 expansion pack" as a separate future product.

## A6: Offline PDF format is the right core format (not an app)

**Assumption:** Buyers prefer an offline-capable PDF over an app, given the no-signal constraint and the 2–4 week creation window available.
**Test:** Free sample response; monitor comments for app requests. If 40%+ of comments request an app instead of a PDF upgrade, reassess format for v2.
**If wrong (v1.0):** Cannot pivot to app within v1.0 window. Add app to backlog. Enhance offline-readiness of PDF (laminate-ready pages, larger font options).
