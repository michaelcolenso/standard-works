# Pricing Hypothesis — PNW Foraging & Mycology Field Guide + Log System

## Tiers

| Tier | Contents | Price | Platform |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free Sample** | 5 species profiles (Chanterelle, Lobster, Chicken of the Woods, Stinging Nettle, Oregon Grape) + 1 safety checklist | $0 | Reddit post / Gumroad |
| **Core Bundle** | 60-page PDF field guide (50 species) + Harvest Log (Excel/Google Sheets) + Seasonal Calendar (PDF) + Spot Tracker template | **$19** | Gumroad / Etsy |
| **Full System** | Everything in Core Bundle + Notion template pack (linked DB) + Recipe & Preservation quick-ref + Priority update access for 12 months | **$37** | Gumroad |
| **Club / Educator License** | Full System × 25 copies + print-ready versions for handouts | **$97** | Gumroad direct |

## Rationale

- **$19 anchors below the $22–$28 range of comparable Etsy mushroom log templates** (275+ sales verified on comparable listings), making it clearly superior value at the same impulse-buy threshold.
- **$37 full system** is still a single digit percentage of what foragers spend on boots, gear, and field guide books annually. Comparable Shroomify Pro subscription is $48/year with no offline ID key.
- **Free sample** is the primary demand-test mechanism: post 5-species PDF to r/foraging and r/mycology, track download count and conversion to paid within 7 days.
- **Club license at $97** targets PNW mycology club education programs (Puget Sound Mycological Society runs 6+ forays per year with 20–40 participants each); one club sale = 5 individual sales equivalent.
- Kill price signal: If free sample gets under 200 downloads in 14 days across both subreddits, pause paid launch and diagnose positioning.

## Comparable market anchors

- Etsy mushroom harvest log templates: $8–$28 (median $15, but no guide integration)
- Arora's *All That the Rain Promises*: $20 used / $28 new (print only, 1991)
- Shroomify Pro: $48/year (app, requires signal, no log system)
- Local mycology club membership (PSMS): $35/year (community only, no field guide included)
