# Open Questions — PNW Foraging & Mycology Field Guide + Log System

These questions must be answered before architecture is locked. Each has a defined resolution path.

## Q1: What are WA and OR state harvest limits for commercial and personal-use foraging on public land?

**Why it matters:** The sustainability section of the guide must cite accurate legal limits per species, not generic advice. Wrong numbers create liability and distrust.

**Resolution path:** Check USDA Forest Service PNW regional office harvest regulations and Washington DNR recreation guidelines. Oregon Department of Forestry has a parallel document. Cross-reference with PSMS published foray guidelines. Target: specific pound limits per species per day, by land type (National Forest vs. State Park vs. BLM).

**Deadline:** Before architecture lock.

## Q2: Is there an existing PNW-specific PDF field guide (combined fungi + plants) already sold on Etsy or Gumroad?

**Why it matters:** If a direct competitor already exists at under $20 with good reviews, differentiation requires a different angle (deeper templates, educator focus, or different species count).

**Resolution path:** Search Etsy for "PNW foraging guide PDF," "Pacific Northwest mushroom identification PDF," "PNW wild edibles guide digital." Note any listings with 50+ sales and comparable scope.

**Deadline:** Before pricing is finalized.

## Q3: What is the standard liability disclaimer language used by published field guides?

**Why it matters:** The product requires legally appropriate disclaimer language. Too weak creates exposure; too strong destroys buyer confidence.

**Resolution path:** Review disclaimer language from 3 published foraging field guides: Arora, Trudell/Ammirati, and one recent self-published guide. Synthesize standard language. Flag for legal review if any novel claim is made.

**Deadline:** Before 03-drafts begins.

## Q4: Can a single PSMS or OMS chapter advisor be secured for a review pass?

**Why it matters:** An expert sign-off enables the "expert-reviewed" claim in marketing copy, which is a top Etsy conversion signal. Without it, the product cannot make that claim.

**Resolution path:** Email PSMS education committee contact. Offer product credit / acknowledgment in exchange for a 2-hour review of species profiles and decision trees. If no response in 14 days, launch without the claim and add it post-launch if an advisor engages.

**Deadline:** Outreach by end of Week 1 of architecture phase.

## Q5: What template format (Excel vs. Google Sheets vs. Notion) should be the primary log deliverable?

**Why it matters:** The answer changes the file format, build complexity, and offline-first compatibility promise.

**Resolution path:** Poll free-sample download audience (one-question form linked from the free PDF). Ask: "Which do you use most for tracking?" with options: Excel, Google Sheets, Notion, Paper notebook. If no poll data available before architecture lock, default to Excel + Google Sheets dual format (both ship; Notion is bonus).

**Deadline:** Architecture lock can proceed with the default; poll result can refine before 05-assets.
